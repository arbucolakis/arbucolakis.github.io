About adarsh

Hi, I am Adarsh!
I am an aeronautical engineer by education, a software developer by profession, and at heart highly and deeply interested in space technology and the vastness of the universe. I have graduated with a degree in aerospace engineering from IIT Madras in 2021, During my graduation I had priviledge to have an opportunity to work as a summer research intern at ISRO that kindled my love for data analytics and software. While I am a software developer today, passion for aerospace and space sciences stays with me as the driving force behind my work.

Currenty I work at Healthcare data science giant, IQVIA, where I help manage and optimize databases for application support for healthcare trials. It's rewarding, and exciting to learn how technology can drive the improvement of patients' outcome. But even while focusing on software, I never forget my roots in aerospace. I always keep up with the latest on space exploration, and I'm always thinking about how best to combine my love for technology and space science.

Beyond work, I am passionate about using technology to make a difference. I founded a non-profit website that uses AI to aid people in navigating local laws, and I organize astronomy events for underprivileged kids to inspire the next generation of scientists and engineers.

I'm always excited to meet like-minded people-to discuss anything from tech, space, or just life as a whole. Feel free to Reach out!
